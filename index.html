Smoking and Poverty

Observations

There is a slight correlation between poverty and smoking. At the higher end of poverty rates there is a distinct increase in smoking. However, the majority of states, there is a consistent pattern with smokers between 14% and 22% and poverty rates ranging from 9% and 21%

In Utah, they have less than 10% rate of smokers.

In West Virgina and Ketucky, more than 25% of people smoke.
